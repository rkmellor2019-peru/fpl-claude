Fix: Add FPL Cookies to Vercel
FPL is blocking anonymous requests. You need to add your session cookies once — they last weeks.

Step 1 — Open fantasy.premierleague.com and log in.
Step 2 — Press F12 to open DevTools → go to the Console tab.
Step 3 — Paste this and press Enter:
document.cookie.split(';').filter(c=>c.trim().match(/^(access_token|refresh_token|pl_profile)=/)).map(c=>c.trim()).join('\n')
Step 4 — Copy the output. You'll see something like:
access_token=eyJ...
refresh_token=eyJ...

Step 5 — Go to your Vercel dashboard → your project → Settings → Environment Variables and add:
FPL_ACCESS_TOKEN = the value after access_token=
FPL_REFRESH_TOKEN = the value after refresh_token=
Step 6 — Redeploy on Vercel (Settings → Deployments → Redeploy), then reload this page.
